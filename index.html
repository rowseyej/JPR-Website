What is 1st & Forever Fitness

At First and Forever, we create forever athletes. We do this by following the proven strength and conditioning principles used by top athletes around the world. Our personalized approach helps define your goals, motivates you to stay engaged, and delivers results.

Meet J.P.

J.P.'s fitness journey began much like that of many trainers - as a passionate athlete. From a young age, he engaged in a wide range of sports, basically any sport that had some type of goal and was competitive. This passion persisted throughout his high school years, where he proudly represented his school as a three-sport athlete. His journey in sports didn't end there; he went on to play football at the division three level.

As an adult, J.P. ventured into the corporate world, but soon realized that his true calling lay in athletics, particularly in strength and conditioning. His path led him to explore various aspects of fitness, from bodybuilding to coaching college football under the guidance of one of the best in the field. This experience inspired J.P. to create a program that would help both youth and adults achieve their fitness, physique, and performance goals.

What sets us apart?

The 1st & Fitness Way

Our Unique Approach:

What sets our training style apart is our commitment to utilizing a blend of techniques and principles. We draw from various disciplines, including powerlifting, strongman training, and mobility work, to craft personalized programs tailored to each client's needs.

Our Mission:

As trainers, our mission is twofold. First, we aim to help youth athletes unlock their full athletic potential. Second, we are dedicated to assisting adults in bridging the gap between their sporting years and the responsibilities of adulthood.

Our Focus:

At 1st & Forever Fitness, we emphasize three key pillars:
  • Strength Training
  • Mobility Enhancement
